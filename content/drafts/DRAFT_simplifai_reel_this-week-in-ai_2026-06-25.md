## The script

Three tracks per beat: **[ON-SCREEN]** big kinetic text · **[SAY]** spoken to camera · **[SHOW]** visual / shot.

### HOOK — 0:00–0:04
- **[ON-SCREEN]** An AI can now answer your **DMs** for you. · + 2 more updates this week
- **[SAY]** "An AI can now answer your customer DMs and book appointments for you. And that's just one of three things that changed in AI this week. Plain English, two minutes."

## Talking-head shot list

**Gear:** Phone is fine. Rear camera (better than selfie cam), 4K or 1080p, 30fps. Horizontal phone? No, shoot vertical 9:16.

| # | Shot | Framing | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Hook to camera | Mid-shot, eyes upper third, head with a little room above | Highest energy take. Look down the lens, not at yourself. |
| 2 | Beat 1 delivery | Same framing | Record full script in one take, then again 2–3 times for safety. |
| 3 | Beat 2 delivery | Same framing | Keep hands free to gesture; it reads as natural. |
| 4 | Beat 3 caveat | Slightly closer (chest-up) | The slight push-in signals "this is the important, honest bit." |
| 5 | CTA to camera | Mid-shot | Warm, a small smile, slightly slower so the ask lands. |

**Screen-recording inserts (capture separately on your phone/desktop):**
- INSERT A — Canva: screen-record typing a prompt and a design generating (~4s usable).
- INSERT B — ChatGPT: screen-record opening Settings → Personalization → Memory (~3s). Blur anything personal.
- INSERT C — Instagram DMs: a short, generic DM thread on a phone (~3s). Use a dummy/test account, no real customer data.

**Setup checklist**
- Light from a window in front of you (face the light, not backlit).
- Background: clean, on-brand if possible (a plain wall, a coral or yellow prop in frame ties it to the brand).
- Audio: record in a quiet room; phone mic close, or a cheap clip mic. Audio matters more than camera.
- Keep all on-screen text and the wordmark inside safe zones: top 220px, bottom 420px, right 180px clear.

## Visual direction (suggested)

**The recognizable series look** (so week 2, 3, 4 are instantly "oh, the Simplifai AI update"):
- The yellow **"THIS WEEK IN AI" pill** top-left on every episode. This is the series brand. Keep it identical each week.
- A big yellow **number** for the count ("3" this week) as the hook focal point.
