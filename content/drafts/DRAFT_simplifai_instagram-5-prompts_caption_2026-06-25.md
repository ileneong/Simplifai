## Caption

5 prompts that replace a $2,000/month marketing hire.

Not because AI is magic. Most of what you'd pay a marketer for monthly is repeatable, and a good prompt makes it repeatable for you. You stay the expert. The prompt just gets you to a first draft in minutes.

Copy these. Fill in the brackets. Save it for Monday.

1. Brand voice doc
"Help me document my brand voice. I do [what you do] for [who you serve]. Here are 3 things I wrote that sound like me: [paste 3]. Describe my tone in 5 traits, 10 words I use, 5 I'd never use, and 2 example sentences in my voice."

2. A month of content
"Act as my content planner. I'm a [trade] serving [customer]. Give me 12 post ideas for 4 weeks, each mapped to one job: teach, build trust, or invite people to work with me. Realistic for posting twice a week."

3. A caption in your voice
"Using this voice: [paste traits from prompt 1], write a caption about [topic]. 5 short sentences, second person, one idea, end with a question. No hashtags or em dashes."

4. One idea into a week
"Here's one thing I want to say: [paste idea]. Repurpose into a 6-slide carousel outline, a 30-second reel script, and 3 caption hooks. Keep my voice consistent."

5. Your customers' own words
"Here are recent reviews and enquiries: [paste 5 to 10, names removed]. Pull the exact phrases they use for their problem and what they wanted. Group into themes. Quote, don't paraphrase."

One rule above all: never paste a customer's personal details into an AI tool. Strip names first. Credible beats fast.

## Why this post is built to grow followers (not just inform)
