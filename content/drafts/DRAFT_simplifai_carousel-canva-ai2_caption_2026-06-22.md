## Caption

If the blank canvas is what stops you from posting, Canva just removed it.

At Canva Create 2026 they launched Canva AI 2.0. Instead of scrolling templates, you describe what you want, by typing or even speaking, and it builds the design for you. The big shift for busy owners:

→ You start from a real draft, not an empty page.
→ Every element stays editable, so you can ask it to swap just the headline or one photo and leave the rest.
→ It learns your brand over time and keeps your work consistent.

One honest note: it's rolling out in stages, so not every account has every feature yet, and the strongest tools sit on paid plans. It also only stays truly on-brand once you've set your brand kit up properly. That part is still on you.

Try it this week: open Canva, and instead of picking a template, describe the post you need in one sentence. See how close it gets.
