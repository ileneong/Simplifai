Why most flop
A carousel isn't a photo album. It's an argument.
Most slides have no job, so people swipe past. The fix is a structure you reuse every time, not better taste.
02 / 06
*
Step 1
Give every slide one job.
Hook, then the problem, then two or three ideas, then a clear ask. One idea per slide, and each one readable on its own.
03 / 06
*
Step 2
Lock three colours and two fonts.
One bold font for headlines, one clean font for the rest. Same logo, same corner, every slide. That repetition is your brand.
04 / 06
*
Step 3
Win the thumbnail.
Slide one is the only slide most people see. One idea, huge type, readable at thumbnail size. If it doesn't stop the scroll, nothing behind it gets read.
