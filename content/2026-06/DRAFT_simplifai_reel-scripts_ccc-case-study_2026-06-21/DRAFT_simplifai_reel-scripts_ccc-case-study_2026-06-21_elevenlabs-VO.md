### ElevenLabs settings (recommended)
- **Model:** Eleven v3 (supports the inline audio tags below). On Turbo/Multilingual v2, delete the
  `[bracketed]` tags — the punctuation pacing still carries the read.
- **Stability:** ~40–50 (Natural). Lower = more expressive but less consistent across reels; keep it
  the same value for all four so the voice matches batch-to-batch.
- **Similarity:** ~75. **Style:** low-to-mid. Pick one voice and reuse it for the whole batch.
- **Pacing:** `...` = a beat / hesitation. `CAPS` = emphasis. Commas and full stops do the rest.
  Keep each block as a single generation so the rhythm holds.
- Disclose AI assistance in every caption (handled in the storyboard doc).

## Reel 9 VO — "The weekend rebuild" (warm, energetic, proud)

```
[warm] I rebuilt a wellness coach's entire content system in a weekend. [confident] Not a post... the SYSTEM.

[thoughtful] She's a real coaching brand, so I'll keep her anonymous. Before, her content was sporadic, and it took hours.

[warm] So we built her a Stack. Her brand voice, her prompts, her templates... so the posts sound like HER, every single time.

[sincere] Then I handed it over. She runs it herself now, a couple of hours a week. I don't want to be the bottleneck.

[confident] That's what "built in a weekend" actually means.
```

## Reel 10 VO — "AI content is cringe? This is AI." (punchy, hot-take energy)

```
[matter-of-fact] Everyone says AI content is generic, soulless, cringe. [confident] Okay. This ENTIRE feed was built with AI.

No "unlock your potential." No robot voice. [thoughtful] Why?

[warm] Because the AI isn't making it up. It's working from her voice, her expertise, her guardrails.

[emphatic] That's the difference between AI that flattens your voice... and AI that protects it.

[confident] Cringe isn't an AI problem. It's a BRIEF problem.
```

## Reel 11 VO — "Why I fired myself as their agency" (quiet, intimate, reflective)

```
[reflective] One of my best clients used to pay me every single month. [pause] I told them to stop. Here's why.

[thoughtful] I was handing them content. Good content. But every month, they needed me again. They never owned it.

[sincere] And one day it hit me... I wasn't building their marketing. I was renting it to them.

[confident] So I changed the whole company. Now I build the SYSTEM, and I hand it over.

[warm] You bring the expertise. You always had it. I just build the thing that lets you run it yourself.
```

## Reel 12 VO — "Good brief vs. bad brief" (clear, demonstrative, building)

```
[matter-of-fact] Same AI, same topic... but watch what one lazy sentence does to your content.

Bad brief: [flat] "write me an Instagram post about mindfulness."

[unimpressed] Generic. Could be anyone. This is the AI everyone complains about.

[confident] Now the Simplifai brief. Voice, audience, one real insight, a guardrail.

[emphatic] Same model. Completely different result. The skill was never the AI... it's knowing what to ASK it.

[confident] The brief is the job.
```

(first line) and the final loop line intact, since the last line loops back to the hook.
