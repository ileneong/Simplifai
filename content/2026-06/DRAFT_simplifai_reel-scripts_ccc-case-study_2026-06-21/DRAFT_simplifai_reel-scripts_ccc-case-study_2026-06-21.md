## Reel 9 — "The weekend rebuild" (flagship case study)
**Pillar:** Show / Proof · **Lane:** C VO · **Scheme:** A Yellow end-cards (`#ffc857`/dark text/coral ✳) · **Accent:** asterisk ✳ · **Mechanic:** Save

**BODY**

**LOOP / CTA (final 3–5s)**

- VO: "That's what 'built in a weekend' actually means." *(loops to hook)*

The point was never to keep her dependent on me. Save this if you'd want yours rebuilt too. *(Made with AI assistance — disclosed.)*
