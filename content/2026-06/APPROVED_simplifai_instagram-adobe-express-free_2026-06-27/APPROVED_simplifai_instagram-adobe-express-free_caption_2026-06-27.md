Most owners think decent graphics need a paid design subscription. Not anymore.

Adobe Express just opened its AI Assistant to the free plan, with no waitlist. You describe the post in plain words, for example "a 4 by 5 promo for my Tuesday workshop," and it builds an editable draft. Swap a photo, tweak the headline, done.

How to start:
1. Set your brand colours and fonts first, so "on-brand" actually means something.
2. Describe one post, not a whole month.
3. Edit the draft. The first version is a starting point, not the finish.

The honest part: it is still a beta, desktop web only for now, and the free tier gives you a small number of AI generations a day. Great for a few posts a week, not batch-producing a month at once.

Source: Adobe's own Express updates, June 2026.
