AI, responsibly
Your AI use policy fits on one page.
Before you scale AI in your business, write down the rules. It takes about ten honest lines.
Why it matters
Trust is the new differentiator.
Customers and staff want to know how you use AI. A clear policy turns a quiet worry into a visible sign of credibility.
Line 1
What you actually use AI for.
Name the jobs. Drafting captions, summarising notes, first-pass research. Clarity here keeps your whole team aligned.
Line 2
What never goes in.
List the data that stays out of every model. Customer details, payment info, anything private or regulated. This line protects you most.
Lines 3 and 4
Who checks it, and how you tell people.
Name who reviews AI output before it ships. Then decide how you disclose AI involvement to your customers.
Your move
Ten honest lines beat a perfect policy you never write.
Open a doc today and draft yours. Save this post as your checklist.
