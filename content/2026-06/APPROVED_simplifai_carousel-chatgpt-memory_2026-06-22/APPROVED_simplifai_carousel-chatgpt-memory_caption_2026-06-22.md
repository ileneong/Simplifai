## Caption

If you retype who your business is every time you open ChatGPT, this one is for you.

Earlier this month OpenAI rebuilt ChatGPT's memory. Two things matter for you as the owner:

1. It now holds roughly double what it used to, so it can actually keep your brand voice, your ideal customer, and your offers in mind across chats.
2. You get clearer controls to see, edit, and delete exactly what it remembers about you.

How to put it to work this week:

→ In one message, tell it plainly who you are and who you serve. Your trade, your customer, your tone.
→ Ask it to remember that.
→ From then on, it starts from your brief instead of a blank box. Less re-explaining, more on-brand drafts.

One honest caveat, because trust matters more than speed: open the memory settings and check what it kept. And never paste a customer's personal details into any AI tool. More memory, same rule.
