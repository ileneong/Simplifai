## 1. ElevenLabs voiceover script (paste this in)

Paste the block below into ElevenLabs Text to Speech. It is written for natural delivery: short sentences, commas and full stops for pacing, no em dashes (em dashes make TTS rush the line). Keep the line breaks, they read as small breaths.

AI that actually does the work for your business is finally here.

But here is the part most people miss.

In May, Anthropic launched Claude for Small Business. It comes with ready made workflows for real jobs. Your monthly books. Cash forecasts. Chasing leads. Even building your marketing assets.

You connect the tools you already use, like Canva, HubSpot, and Google Workspace. You pick the job. Claude does it.

And here is the part that actually matters. It asks for your approval before anything sends, posts, or pays. You stay in control. The AI just clears the busywork.

The honest part. It needs a paid Claude plan, and it can only touch what you already can. So start it on the small stuff first.

Save this for when you are ready to let AI do the work, not just draft it.

**Approx. read time:** 36 to 40 seconds at a natural pace.

### Suggested ElevenLabs settings
- **Voice:** a warm, credible, mid-range voice (think trusted friend who knows their stuff, not a hype announcer). Avoid overly "broadcast" or breathy voices.
- **Stability:** ~50 percent (steady but not flat).
- **Similarity / clarity:** ~75 percent.
- **Style exaggeration:** low (0 to 15 percent), keep it grounded, this is a trust topic.
- **Speed:** default, or very slightly slower. Let the one word lines ("Cash forecasts.") land.
- Generate, then listen for the line "It asks for your approval before anything sends, posts, or pays." That is the emotional beat, regenerate if it rushes it.
