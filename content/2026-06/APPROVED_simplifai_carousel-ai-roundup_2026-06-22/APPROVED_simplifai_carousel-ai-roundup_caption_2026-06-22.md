## Caption

Three AI updates landed this month that actually change how you market your business. Here's the plain-English version.

1. ChatGPT's memory roughly doubled. Tell it who you are and who you serve once, and it holds that across chats. A lot less re-explaining.

2. Canva AI 2.0 builds from a sentence. Describe the design you want and it makes it, fully editable. The blank canvas is gone.

3. Meta added AI to your dashboard and DMs. One assistant reads your numbers and flags what's working. Another answers common DMs and books appointments, now inside Instagram.

New tools, same rule we always come back to: they're useful, but you stay in the driver's seat, and a customer's personal details never go into an AI tool.
