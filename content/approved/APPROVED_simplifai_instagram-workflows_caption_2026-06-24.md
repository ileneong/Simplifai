## Caption

Look at your week and you'll see the same jobs come back around. Answering the same questions. Chasing the same leads. Asking for reviews you keep meaning to ask for. Writing the post you didn't have time to write.

It's not that you're disorganised. It's that a small business runs on a handful of repeating workflows, and right now most of them live in your head.

That's exactly where AI earns its place. Not replacing your judgement, just taking the first pass:

→ Customer support: a draft reply, ready for you to send or fix
→ Lead generation: enquiries captured and sorted, not lost in the inbox
→ Feedback and reviews: a polite ask that goes out without you remembering
→ Content and social: a week of captions started, not from a blank page
→ Bookings: reminders that send themselves
→ Follow-ups: the quote, the invoice, the check-in that usually slips

You stay in control. You bring the expertise. The system handles the repeating part so a few hours a week is genuinely enough.

Which of these eats the most of your week? Tell us below, and save this for when you're ready to set one up.
