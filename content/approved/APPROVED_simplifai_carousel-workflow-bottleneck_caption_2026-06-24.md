## Caption

The most expensive job in your business is the one only you can do.

For a lot of owners, that job is the inbox. Every enquiry, every quote, every "are you available" sits and waits, because you are the only person who can answer it in a way that sounds like you. So leads go cold, and you end up writing replies at 10pm.

You do not need to hire someone to fix that. You need a system. Here is one operational workflow you can build this week:

1. Capture. Pull every enquiry into one place so nothing slips through.
2. Draft. Use one prompt, built on your FAQs, pricing and tone, to write a first-draft reply for each one.
3. Approve. You read it, tweak it, send it. Two minutes instead of twenty.

You stay in control of every word. The work just stops bottlenecking on you. And the whole thing costs a fraction of the part-time hire you were dreading.

Save this for the next time your inbox runs away from you. What is the one task in your business that always waits for you? Tell us below.
