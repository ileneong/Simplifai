# Reel Script — "How to Prompt Like a Pro"

**Format:** Talking-head reel (Instagram / TikTok)
**Length:** ~45–55 seconds
**Goal:** Educate — give owners one repeatable framework they can use today
**Disclosure:** Caption to include "Made with a little help from AI 🤖"

---

## Script

Most people talk to AI like it's a search bar — that's why they get boring answers. Here's how to prompt like a pro in four moves. One: give it a role. Don't say "write a caption," say "you're a marketing copywriter for a busy café" — the AI shows up differently when it knows who it's being. Two: feed it context. Your brand voice, your audience, what you actually sell — the AI doesn't know your business, so tell it, because five sentences of context beats a clever one-liner every time. Three: be specific about the output — not "give me ideas," but "give me three caption options, under 30 words, friendly but not salesy." Tell it the format and the limits. And four: treat the first answer as a draft, not the final — say "make it warmer," "cut the jargon," "try a bolder hook." The magic isn't one perfect prompt, it's the back-and-forth. Role, context, task, iterate — save this for your next caption, and follow for the system that makes marketing a few hours a week, not a full-time job.
