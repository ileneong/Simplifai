*What's working organically for rival creators (YouTube, ranked by public views), read against Simplifai's edge. ## TL;DR — the three things Simplifai should take from this
1. **They all teach people to START an AI business; nobody helps you RUN the one you already have.** Every top video is "build and sell AI agents / start an AI agency / make $X." Simplifai's audience already owns a business. That gap is the whole opening.
2. **"No code" is a bait-and-switch.** Their beginner hooks promise no coding, then open Claude Code or n8n. Simplifai can own genuinely non-technical, owner-run AI and call out the gap honestly.
3. **They chase tool-of-the-week churn** ("just dropped," "destroys," "kills n8n"). Simplifai wins by being calm and durable: a vetted system that still works next month, not the tool that trended today.

- **Top themes:**
  - *Start a business / make money:* "How I Build $5,000+ Websites in Minutes with AI" (292K), "How to Start an AI Business in 2026" (152K), "10 AI Tools to Make Your First Million" (107K).
  - *Beginner, no-code:* "Build & Sell AI Agents: Ultimate Beginner's Guide" (196K), "Your First AI Voice Agent in 18 Minutes (No Coding)" (122K).

- **Simplifai read:** sells a new *career* (run an agency), not help for the business you have. Technical underneath the "no code" promise. No vertical, heavy on income framing. Simplifai is the mirror image: existing owner, wellness-led, no code, no income fantasy.

3. **You don't need the tool that dropped today**
   - **Angle:** Calm and durable beats tool-of-the-week churn.
