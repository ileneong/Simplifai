## Top 3 this week

### 1. Calendly now connects directly to Claude and ChatGPT

- **What changed:** Calendly officially supports connection to Claude and ChatGPT, so you can book appointments from inside an AI conversation without switching apps. Tell Claude "book a 60-minute consult with Maria next Tuesday at 11am" and Calendly handles the scheduling automatically.
- **Source + date:** Calendly, calendly.com/help/connect-calendly-to-your-ai-tools, 13 July 2026
- **So what for the owner:** If you already use Claude or ChatGPT to plan your week, you can now stay in that same conversation to book client appointments. No switching between apps, no copy-pasting names and times. For a health coach, therapist, or studio owner who is appointment-driven, this cuts a repetitive step out of daily admin.
- **Honest caveat:** You need a Calendly account and need to set up the connection through Calendly's integrations settings first. It works best if Claude or ChatGPT is already a regular part of how you plan your week. If you only open AI tools occasionally, the setup time may not pay off yet.
- **Content angle:** Carousel · System teaching · hook: "You can now book a client without leaving your AI chat."

### 2. Notion Agents can now manage your calendar and send meeting invites

- **What changed:** Notion Agents gained built-in calendar tools on 16 July. From within the Notion chat interface, you can now ask your agent to read your schedule, find available slots, send meeting invites, and surface conflicts. An iOS app for Notion Agents also launched on 8 July, so you can trigger agents from your phone between sessions.
- **Source + date:** Notion, notion.com/releases/2026-07-16 (calendar tools) and notion.com/releases/2026-07-08 (iOS app), July 2026
- **So what for the owner:** A coach or practitioner who runs their business inside Notion can now say "find a free 45-minute slot next week for a discovery call and send an invite to this email" and have it handled without opening Google Calendar, Calendly, or a separate scheduling tool. The iOS app means you can trigger this between client sessions, not just at your desk.
- **Honest caveat:** Notion Agents are available on Notion Business plan and above for full functionality. Check your current Notion plan before expecting this to work. The calendar connection also depends on linking your Google Calendar or Outlook to Notion first.
- **Content angle:** Carousel · System teaching · hook: "Your Notion just learned to manage your diary."

---

### 3. Adobe Express AI Assistant can now swap text inside images on the free plan

- **What changed:** Adobe Express AI Assistant (in beta) gained two new abilities on 16 July: replace text that appears inside an image, like an event name on a flyer or a headline on a poster, with new text that automatically matches the original font style; and adjust animations in designs using plain-English instructions ("make the fade-in slower"). Both features are available on the free plan.
- **Source + date:** Adobe, helpx.adobe.com/express/web/whats-new/release-notes.html, 16 July 2026
- **So what for the owner:** A wellness studio that creates a new promo graphic each month can update an existing design by typing "change 'July Workshop' to 'August Retreat'" rather than rebuilding the layout from scratch. No subscription upgrade needed. The style matching means the new text looks like it was always there.
- **Honest caveat:** AI Assistant is in beta and text matching works best on clean, poster-style fonts. Heavily decorative or handwritten fonts may not replicate perfectly. Always check the result before using it on a post.
- **Content angle:** Single or Reel · Myth/reframe ("you don't have to rebuild your graphics every month") · hook: "Your old graphics just got a second life."
