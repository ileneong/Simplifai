# Simplifai Competitor Ads — 2026-06-28

*What rivals are running in the Meta Ad Library, read against Simplifai's edge. Researched with AI; ads observed live via single-ad permalinks on 2026-06-28 (region defaulted to Singapore). This is a **single-competitor sample** (HighLevel / GoHighLevel) built to demonstrate the format; a full run sweeps the whole watch list.*

## TL;DR — the three things Simplifai should take from this
1. **GoHighLevel sells speed and agency margin, never trust.** Their live angles are "a website faster than coffee" and "rebill your clients." Nobody is selling *credible, owner-controlled* AI. That lane is wide open and it is exactly Simplifai's.
2. **Their loudest angle is agency-facing** ("upgrade so you can rebill your clients"). That is the very model Simplifai positions against. Speak to the owner who is tired of paying an agency, not to the agency.
3. **Speed-hype is the reframe target.** "In minutes / faster than coffee" is begging for Simplifai's honest counter: a real system you run in a few hours a week, not a magic-button promise.

## Teardowns

### HighLevel (GoHighLevel)
- **Who & what:** All-in-one agency/SMB CRM, marketing, automation and now an AI website builder ("AI Studio"). Sells to agencies and resellers more than to end owners.
- **Dominant hook(s):**
  - *Margin angle:* "You're currently paying your clients' usage fees, but when you upgrade to Unlimited, you can rebill them." / headline "Many growing agencies charge for this."
  - *Speed angle:* "Describe what you want. Get a working website, landing page or form in minutes." with six rotating headlines: "Your website, in the time of a coffee," "Type a sentence, get a website," "Show AI a screenshot, get a site," "Your tech stack just got lighter."
- **The offer:** Free to try with a HighLevel trial (speed angle); "save 50% on Unlimited or Pro, offer ends Sunday" (margin angle). CTA: Sign Up, to gohighlevel.com.
- **Proof used:** Almost none. No testimonials, no numbers, no logos in these creatives. Pure promise.
- **Objection handled:** "Building is slow and technical" (→ minutes, free, screenshot-to-site) and "another tool to add" (→ "your tech stack just got lighter"). The margin ad handles "why upgrade" with "you are leaving money on the table."
- **Longevity:** The speed/AI Studio ad has run since **5 May 2026** (~7 weeks) with **six headline variants** = a proven, hard-optimised angle. The rebilling videos launched **22–23 Jun 2026** in two near-identical variants = a fresh, actively-tested push.
- **Simplifai read:** Everything here is **feature-led and speed-led**, sold to **agencies**. There is no trust, no voice, no "stays like you," no vertical. Gaps Simplifai owns: (a) the owner who does not want an agency at all; (b) credibility over speed; (c) wellness specificity. Do not match the "faster than coffee" hype — reframe it.

## Angles for Simplifai's own ads
1. **Own it, don't rent it**
   - **Angle:** You do not need an agency or ten tools. You need a system you own.
   - **Why it's open:** GoHighLevel's headline angle literally sells agencies on rebilling owners. Simplifai talks to that owner instead.
   - **Hook starter:** "Stop renting your marketing from an agency. Own the system instead."
   - **Proof Simplifai can stand on:** the Academy outcome, a working AI system plus a 4-week calendar and a brand-voice doc, run in a few hours a week.
2. **Fast is easy, trustworthy is the work**
   - **Angle:** Speed is cheap now. Sounding like you, and being true, is the hard part that wins clients.
   - **Why it's open:** every rival ad sells speed and zero of them sell credibility.
   - **Hook starter:** "AI can write your post in seconds. Making it sound like you, and be true, is the real work."
   - **Proof Simplifai can stand on:** every Simplifai output is checked against the brand voice and compliance before it ships.
3. **Generic AI does not know your rules**
   - **Angle:** Off-the-shelf AI does not know what a wellness business can and cannot claim. Yours should.
   - **Why it's open:** none of the rivals speak to a vertical, let alone wellness regulation.
   - **Hook starter:** "Generic AI does not know what a wellness business is allowed to say. Your system should."
   - **Proof Simplifai can stand on:** wellness-tuned guardrails built in (TGA, FDA, mental-health language).
