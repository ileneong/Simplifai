## Top 3 this week

### 1. Google Gemini now connects to your Google Business Profile

- **What changed:** Business owners can link their Google Business Profile to the Gemini app in a single tap. Once connected, Gemini can read your real customer reviews, common questions, and performance data, so it can help you draft review replies, write FAQ content, and generate ideas grounded in what your actual customers say.
- **Source + date:** Google, "Save time and grow your business with new Gemini tools," blog.google, June 10, 2026. Rollout confirmed by 9to5Google, June 10, 2026.
- **So what for the owner:** You can paste a negative review into Gemini and ask for a calm, professional reply in your tone. You can ask it to turn your most common customer questions into a FAQ page. You no longer need to re-explain your business every session because it pulls context from your Profile directly.
- **Honest caveat:** Rolling out gradually across the Gemini app this month. Not available in the EEA or UK yet. Only works for owners who manage a single Business Profile. If you manage multiple locations or are in an agency setup, you are locked out for now. No confirmed cost above a free Gemini account, but this has not been fully clarified.
- **Content angle:** Carousel · System teaching · hook: "Your Google reviews just became your AI briefing doc."

### 2. ChatGPT's model picker finally speaks plain English, and its memory got a rebuild

- **What changed:** On June 10, 2026, OpenAI replaced the confusing "Thinking Standard / Thinking Extended / Thinking Heavy" labels with six plain-language options: Instant, Medium, High, Extra High, Pro Standard, and Pro Extended. On June 4-5, 2026, OpenAI also shipped "Dreaming V3," a rebuilt memory system with roughly double the capacity that reads across past conversations automatically rather than requiring you to manually save things.
- **Source + date:** OpenAI, ChatGPT release notes (help.openai.com/en/articles/6825453-chatgpt-release-notes), June 4 and June 10, 2026.
- **So what for the owner:** You can now pick thinking depth by task without a decoder ring. Quick Instagram caption? Instant. Full content strategy with a brief? High. And with the expanded memory, ChatGPT can hold your brand voice, your audience, and your offers across sessions more reliably, so you stop re-explaining your business every time you open it.
- **Honest caveat:** The expanded memory is for Plus and Pro subscribers only. Free users get Instant and Medium. The bigger memory pool also means it is worth reviewing what ChatGPT has stored about you, especially if you have ever pasted anything sensitive. Never put customer personal data or confidential business information into any AI tool.
- **Content angle:** Carousel · System teaching · hook: "Stop re-explaining your brand to ChatGPT. Here's what actually changed."

### 3. Meta's Business Agent is now live inside Instagram DMs

- **What changed:** Meta launched its Business Agent globally on WhatsApp, Instagram Direct, and Messenger on June 3, 2026. The agent can answer common customer questions, recommend products from your catalog, book appointments, qualify leads, and hand off to a human at a point the owner defines. Over one million businesses used it in pilot markets (India, Mexico, Brazil) before the global rollout.
- **Source + date:** Meta, "Be There for Every Customer With Meta Business Agent," about.fb.com/news/2026/06/meta-business-agent, June 3, 2026. Also covered by TechCrunch, June 3, 2026.
- **So what for the owner:** A customer DMs your Instagram at 11pm asking about your services. The Business Agent can reply with relevant details, ask qualifying questions, and book a discovery call before you are even awake. For owners who receive regular DMs but struggle to respond quickly, this closes a real gap.
- **Honest caveat:** Still waitlist-gated. Most accounts will not get immediate access. Requires WhatsApp Business or Instagram Pro. Pricing is confirmed as coming later. An AI speaking for your brand without careful setup and clear human handoff points can work against the trust you are building with customers. It needs to be treated as a first responder, not a replacement.
- **Content angle:** Single or Reel · Honest limits · hook: "Meta's AI can now answer your customer DMs. Here's when to use it, and when not to."
