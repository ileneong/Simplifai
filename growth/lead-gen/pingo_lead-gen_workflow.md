# Pingo Lead-Gen Workflow — Instagram (CCC community as a discovery funnel)

*Last updated: 2026-06-01*

A repeatable, compliant way to find **Pingo's own prospects** by working the corner of Instagram where Pingo's clients already live. CCC (Conscious Connections Consultancy) is the first source community; the same workflow reuses across any client niche.

---

## Who we're looking for (ICP)

Wellness, coaching, and mindfulness **business owners and creators** who:

- run a solo practice or small team (≈ 2–15 staff),
- **post their own content** (so they care about marketing and are doing it themselves),
- are genuine experts but **inconsistent or invisible online**, and
- are **active in community** — they comment, reply, show up for peers.

This is the person Pingo is built for: *"You bring the expertise. We bring the system."* They are **peers of CCC**, not CCC's coaching clients.

---

## Hard guardrails (non-negotiable)

1. **No scraping, no bots, no bulk/automated DMs.** Discovery is manual and outreach is individually written. This protects the account from restriction and is the only approach that fits a relationship-led brand. (Meta does not offer a compliant export of follower/engager profiles anyway.)
2. **Reach out from Pingo's own Instagram account — never CCC's.** We *discover* through CCC's network; the DM is Pingo introducing itself. → *Prerequisite: Pingo needs an active IG handle with a complete, credible bio before outreach starts.*
3. **Never poach CCC's clients.** Targets are peer practitioners. If someone looks like an actual CCC coaching client/lead, they're disqualified (see rubric).
4. **Pingo voice: warm, credible, empowering.** Lead with value, not a pitch. **Never paste a URL or pingo.co** — Pingo has no website yet.
5. **No PII or client data** ever pasted into any tool.

---

## Where to find warm leads (all manual, all in-app)

Work these sources in roughly this order — top ones are warmest:

1. **Peers engaging with CCC's posts.** Open recent CCC posts → tap the likers and commenters → keep only accounts whose **bio reads like a business** (see signals below), not consumers/clients.
2. **Mutuals & near-mutuals.** Accounts CCC follows, and accounts that follow CCC, who are themselves practitioners.
3. **Hashtag communities CCC sits in.** Browse *recent* (not top) posts under tags like `#consciouscoaching` `#mindfulnesscoach` `#wellnesscoach` `#consciousleadership` `#relationshipcoach`. Real, recent posters only.
4. **"Suggested / similar accounts."** Tap the ⌄ on CCC's profile and on each qualified lead — Instagram surfaces look-alike accounts.
5. **Collab & tag partners.** Accounts tagged in CCC's posts, or that co-host/feature with CCC.
6. **Engaged commenters on peer accounts.** Once you've found a few good peers, mine *their* commenters for more of the same.

### Reading a "business owner" signal from a bio
Look for: **"coach / founder / practitioner / facilitator"**, named **services or offers**, a **booking link or Linktree**, a business (not personal) name, or "DM to work with me". Personal/diary accounts with none of these are usually consumers — skip.

## Cadence

Time-box it so it stays sustainable:

- **30 minutes × 3 sessions/week** → roughly **15–20 new candidates/week** logged.
- Keep discovery and outreach in separate passes — find/score/log first, warm up and DM second. Batching protects the voice and avoids rushed, generic messages.

---

## The per-lead loop

```
SPOT  →  SCORE  →  LOG  →  WARM UP  →  DM OPENER  →  TRACK  →  FOLLOW-UP
```

1. **Spot** a candidate from a source above.
2. **Score** them against `pingo_lead-qualification-rubric.md`. Below threshold or any disqualifier → don't log, move on.
3. **Log** qualified leads in `pingo_lead-tracker.csv` (`outreach_status = not-contacted`).
4. **Warm up** *before* any DM: follow them, and leave **one genuine comment or like** on recent content. No engagement → no DM. Set `warmed_up = Y`.
5. **DM opener** from `pingo_outreach-dm-templates.md` — personalise the first line to their actual content. Set `outreach_status = opener-sent`, fill `last_touch_date`.
6. **Track** every reply and update `outreach_status`.
7. **Follow up** per the template rules: **max 2 follow-ups**, spaced out; then graceful close. No response → `no-response`, stop.

---

## What "good" looks like

A handful of real conversations a week with the right people beats a hundred ignored cold DMs. Success = `in-conversation` and `booked` counts trending up, and a clean tracker you can review weekly. Quality of fit and quality of message win — always.
