# Caption Formulas

## The Hook-Value-CTA Formula
1. **Hook** — Grab attention in the first line (question, bold statement, surprising fact)
2. **Value** — Deliver useful, credible information (2–5 sentences)
3. **CTA** — Clear call to action (ask a question, link in bio, save this post)

## The Storytelling Formula
1. **Relatable problem** — Start with a pain point the audience recognises
2. **Turning point** — Introduce the insight or solution
3. **Outcome** — Paint the picture of what's possible
4. **CTA** — Invite engagement

## The Education Formula
1. **Surprising fact or question**
2. **The explanation (short, jargon-free)**
3. **Practical takeaway**
4. **CTA to save or share**

## House Style
- **Length:** stay under Instagram's 2,200-character limit (emoji count as 2 characters). Count before shipping and leave ~50 characters of headroom.
- **Emojis:** use some, but not excessively — keep them relevant. A few as visual anchors (list markers, a key warning, the closing CTA), never decorative strings.

## Compliance Reminders
- Avoid: "This will..." / "Guaranteed to..." / "Cures..."
- Use: "May support..." / "Research suggests..." / "Many people find..."
- Always add: "Consult your healthcare professional" for clinical claims
