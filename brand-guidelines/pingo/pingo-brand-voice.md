# Pingo Brand Voice & Tone Guidelines

## Voice Pillars
1. **Warm** — Human, approachable, genuinely caring
2. **Credible** — Evidence-informed, authoritative, trustworthy
3. **Empowering** — Motivating, community-building, educational

## Tone Spectrum
Pingo's tone shifts depending on context:
- **Educational content:** more authoritative, data-led
- **Community content:** warmer, conversational, relatable
- **Promotional content:** confident but never pushy

## Language Rules
### Use
- "may support", "research suggests", "can help"
- Active voice
- Plain English — no unnecessary jargon
- Second person ("you", "your")

### Avoid
- Absolute health claims ("will cure", "guaranteed to")
- Pseudoscience
- Fearmongering or anxiety-inducing language
- Empty inspirational quotes without substance

## Pingo's Own Social Voice
When posting as Pingo (not a client):
- Speak to wellness business owners, not end consumers
- Position as knowledgeable industry partner, not just a service provider
- Share insights, trends, and behind-the-scenes agency perspective
